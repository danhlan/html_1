Part 2 · Audience & Market Source: ICP v0.2 §Brand Voice
How we sound in copy

Brand voice: honest seller talking to fellow seller.

✓ DO
  • Direct, concrete, specific numbers — "30 books/month" không phải "nhiều books"
  • Outcome-focused language — time saved, books published, revenue per book, Amazon ranking
  • Mention pain points by name — "nonsense word in grids", "Canva lag with 200 pages"
  • Skimmable structure — short paragraphs, bullets, bold key phrases
  • Honest admissions — "Không phải ai dùng cũng ra $10k. Nhưng 3 book/tháng? Realistic."
✗ BANNED WORDS
  • killer · insane · crazy good
  • game-changer · no-brainer · total game changer
  • 6-figure income · passive income empire · make $10k/month easily
  • guaranteed success · anyone can do this
  • secret method · loophole · Amazon doesn't want you to know
  • world's best · ultimate · complete (without backing)

These trigger skepticism in modern W+ buyers. Honest tier (Cloudorka, Helium 10) outperforms hype tier in long-term LTV.

What we learned from launch 1

Insight: 0 sale = segment mismatch + pricing mismatch.

162
Hops tuần 1
0 sales · EPC $0.00
Root cause breakdown
  • Sales page viết cho Segment B (Veteran) — nhưng W+ traffic 70% là Segment A (Newbie). Landing vào thấy "không phải cho tôi".
  • Pricing $79 FE ở vùng awkward middle — Segment A do dự > $50 nếu không có brand trust.
  • JV page hoàn toàn trống — affiliate landing → bounce 95%. Traffic hiện tại là marketplace browse random, không phải warm.
  • Demo video off-page (YouTube link) — mất 30-50% buyer intent ngay khúc giữa sales page.
Implication cho launch 2+

Copy

Viết primarily cho Segment A (newbie language). Thêm 1-2 section cho Segment B nhưng không chiếm trọng tâm.

Pricing

Start ở $17-27 FE để match Segment A budget tolerance. Anchor OTO1/OTO2 lên sau.

Positioning hook

Outcome-focused không feature-focused. "Publish 5 wordsearch books this weekend" > "AI-powered grid generator with 10 fonts".

How we decide what to build next

Dual-mode catalog: spine + opportunism, not either-or.

MODE 1 · SPINE ~70%

The Puzzle Suite for KDP sellers

Default mode. Ổn định. Build engine puzzle generators và adjacent activity tools.

Why
  • Same buyer audience → strong cross-sell
  • Leveraged engine architecture → build faster
  • Defensible positioning → brand narrative rõ
  • Predictable build cadence cho team 3 người
MODE 2 · OPPORTUNISTIC ~30%

Trend-driven products

Flexible. Upside. Khi Blake monitor được trend KDP/low-content có signal đủ mạnh → build parallel.

Triggers
  • Search demand spike trên Amazon
  • Seasonality window 6-8 tuần ahead
  • Viral niche trong KDP community
  • AI publishing wave mới
Lý do dual mode: Pure spine quá rigid — bỏ lỡ trend windows. Pure opportunistic quá chaotic — không build catalog narrative, không cross-sell. Hybrid là sustainable cho team 3 người với parallel workload.
